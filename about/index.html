    About me

    I’m a 2nd-year Ph.D student at Okumura-Takamura group, Tokyo Institute of Technology, supervised by Prof. Manabu Okumura. Previously I received my master’s degree from Tokyo Institute of Technology under the supervision of Prof. Hiroya Takamura. My research interest is natural language generation with neural networks and I’m particularly interested in generating text from non-linguistic data. Also, I am currently working on machine translation as a research engineer at NTT docomo.

    Research Interests

    • Neural Language Generation
    • Data-to-Text Generation
    • Machine Translation
